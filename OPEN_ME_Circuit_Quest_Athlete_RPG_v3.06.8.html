The Circuit Path

Every athlete begins somewhere small: a driveway, a school gym, a pool lane, a track lane, or an empty field. In Circuit Quest, the real player records honest work. The character turns that work into fantasy performance through traits, gear, factions, and training history.

You can travel to every realm. You may not be ready to compete there yet. Visit, train, shop, scout the requirements, and build toward the championship circuit.

Tip: positions and events belong to sports. Body build belongs to the athlete.
